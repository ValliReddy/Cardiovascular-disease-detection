Combining cutting-edge machine learning with user-friendly insights, it empowers individuals and healthcare providers to detect potential heart issues early, ensuring timely interventions. Features

This application leverages advanced AI and machine learning algorithms to predict cardiovascular diseases by analyzing ECG and biometric data. By monitoring heart activity and vital signs, it provides early warnings for potential risks.

PREDICTION USING ECG

Cardiovascular disease prediction using ECG focuses on analyzing the heart's electrical activity to detect abnormalities like arrhythmias, ischemia, or heart block. Advanced algorithms process ECG waveforms to identify subtle changes that may indicate potential risks.

PREDICTION USING BIOMETRICS

Cardiovascular disease prediction using biometric characteristics involves analyzing factors like blood pressure, heart rate, oxygen saturation, cholesterol levels, and body mass index (BMI). These metrics provide insights into the overall health of the cardiovascular system.
